<p><p>The flu diagnosis market is primarily categorized into three types: Traditional Diagnostic Tests, Molecular Diagnostic Tests, and Other Molecular Tests. Traditional tests, like rapid antigen assays and culture methods, offer quick results but may lack sensitivity. Molecular Diagnostic Tests, such as PCR and RT-PCR, provide high accuracy and detect viral RNA, allowing for early identification of the virus. Other Molecular Tests include newer techniques and methodologies like next-generation sequencing that enhance detection capabilities, facilitating better management of influenza outbreaks.</p></p>
