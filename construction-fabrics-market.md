<p><p>The construction fabrics market comprises various materials including PVC, ETFE, and PTFE, each offering unique benefits for architectural applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and versatile, commonly used for tensioned structures due to its durability. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is lightweight, transparent, and provides excellent thermal performance, making it ideal for roofs and facades. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its high resistance to chemicals and UV rays, enhancing the longevity of structures. Together, these materials enhance design flexibility and functionality in construction.</p></p>
