<p><p>High-flow Tracheal Oxygenation (HFTO) is an advanced therapeutic technique used to deliver high-flow oxygen directly to patients with respiratory distress or failure. This method enhances oxygenation by providing a continuous flow of humidified oxygen, improving gas exchange and reducing the work of breathing. HFTO has gained prominence in both emergency and critical care settings due to its efficacy in treating conditions like acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS) and other hypoxemic respiratory failures.</p><p>The HFTO market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by an increase in respiratory diseases, an aging population, and advancements in medical technology.
