<p><strong>The Home Electric Vehicle Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Charger</li><li>AC Charger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home electric vehicle charger market primarily consists of two types: DC chargers and AC chargers. DC chargers, or direct current fast chargers, provide rapid charging by delivering high power directly to the vehicle’s battery, allowing for fast replenishment, typically found in commercial settings. AC chargers, or alternating current chargers, are slower and more prevalent for home use, converting AC power from the grid to a form usable by the vehicle, making them ideal for overnight charging at residential locations.</p></p>
