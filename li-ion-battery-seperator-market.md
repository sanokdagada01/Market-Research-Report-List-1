<p><p>The Li-ion battery separator market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes: </p><p>1. **Microporous** separators allow lithium-ion transport while preventing dendrite formation. </p><p>2. **Nonwovens** provide flexibility and strength, enhancing thermal stability. </p><p>3. **Ion-Exchange Membranes** facilitate ion movement while blocking unwanted reactions. </p><p>4. **Supported Liquid Membranes** combine liquid and solid phases for improved conductivity. </p><p>5. **Other** types include advanced materials and innovations catering to specific performance needs in battery applications. Each type contributes to overall battery efficiency and safety.</p></p>
