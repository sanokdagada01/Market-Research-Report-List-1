<p><p>Sports Management Software operates primarily in two market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering control and customization, but requiring substantial upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet, promoting flexibility, scalable pricing, and ease of updates. This model typically reduces IT burdens and enables real-time collaboration among users, making it increasingly popular among sports organizations seeking efficient management solutions.</p></p>
