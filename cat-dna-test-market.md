<p><p>The cat DNA test market is primarily divided into two types: swab tests and blood tests. In contrast, blood tests require a veterinarian to draw blood to analyze the DNA, providing more comprehensive genetic information. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and needs for identifying breed, health traits, and ancestry in cats.</p></p>
