Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP) is favored for its resorbable properties in bone grafting. Monocalcium Phosphate Monohydrate (MCPM) and Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP) serve as biocompatible materials in dental and orthopedic applications. Other variants include specialized formulations for targeted therapeutic uses, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
