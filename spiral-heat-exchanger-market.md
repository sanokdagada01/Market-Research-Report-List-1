<p><p>A spiral heat exchanger is a type of heat exchanger that features a spiral design, allowing fluids to flow in a coiled configuration. This unique design promotes efficient heat transfer, as the fluids flow in close proximity and through a large surface area while minimizing pressure drops. Spiral heat exchangers are widely used in various industries, including chemical processing, power generation, and food processing, owing to their ability to handle viscous fluids and facilitate effective heat exchange.</p><p>The Spiral Heat Exchanger Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
