<p><p>The Universal Couplings market comprises various types, including Needle Bearing Universal Joints and Plain Bearing Universal Joints. Needle Bearing Universal Joints utilize needle bearings for improved load capacity and reduced friction, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, Plain Bearing Universal Joints are simpler, employing standard bearings without needles, suitable for lower-speed applications where cost and simplicity are prioritized. Both types serve crucial roles in transferring torque and motion between non-aligned shafts across diverse industries, enhancing mechanical efficiency.</p></p>
