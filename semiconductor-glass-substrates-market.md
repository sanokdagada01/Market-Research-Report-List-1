<p><p>The semiconductor glass substrates market is primarily divided into two types: alkali glass and alkali-free glass. Alkali glass, containing sodium and potassium, offers cost-effectiveness and thermal stability, making it suitable for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, alkali-free glass, devoid of these elements, provides superior thermal and chemical resistance, essential for high-performance electronic devices. Both types cater to different requirements in semiconductor manufacturing, driving advancements in technology and expanding their applicability across industries such as electronics and photonics.</p></p>
