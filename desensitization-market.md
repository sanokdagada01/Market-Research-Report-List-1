<p><strong>The Desensitization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Immunotherapy</li><li>Sublingual Immunotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desensitization market types include subcutaneous immunotherapy (SCIT) and sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT). SCIT involves administering allergen extracts via injection to gradually build tolerance, typically requiring visits to healthcare providers. In contrast, SLIT delivers allergens through tablets or drops placed under the tongue, allowing for self-administration and greater convenience. Both methods aim to alleviate allergic reactions by retraining the immune system, and their markets are growing due to increasing allergy prevalence and rising awareness of allergy management options.</p></p>
