<p><p>The special effect pigments market includes two primary types: metallic and pearlescent pigments. Metallic pigments provide a shiny, reflective surface, enhancing the visual appeal of coatings, plastics, and cosmetics. These pigments typically consist of fine metal particles that create a shimmering effect. In contrast, pearlescent pigments offer a color-shifting, iridescent quality, resembling the sheen of pearls. These pigments are often used in cosmetics, automotive coatings, and packaging to provide depth and a unique aesthetic, appealing to various industries.</p></p>
