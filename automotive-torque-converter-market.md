<p><p>The automotive torque converter market is primarily divided into two types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters include a single set of impellers, offering efficient power transfer and simplicity, ideal for standard vehicles. In contrast, multistage torque converters utilize multiple sets of impellers to enhance performance and efficiency, providing better torque multiplication and smoother acceleration for high-performance or heavy-duty vehicles. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing cost, performance, and driving experience.</p></p>
