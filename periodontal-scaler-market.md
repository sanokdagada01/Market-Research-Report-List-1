<p><ul><li>Mechanical Scalers</li><li>Power Driven (Oscillatory) Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The periodontal scaler market includes two primary types: mechanical scalers and power-driven (oscillatory) scalers. Mechanical scalers are manually operated tools used to remove plaque and tartar from tooth surfaces and periodontal pockets. In contrast, power-driven (oscillatory) scalers utilize vibrations or oscillations to enhance the efficiency of plaque removal, often leading to quicker and more effective cleaning. Both types are essential in periodontal therapy, contributing to improved oral health and patient outcomes.</p></p>
