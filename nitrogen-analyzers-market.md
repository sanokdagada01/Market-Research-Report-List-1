<p><strong>The Nitrogen Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen analyzers market consists of various methods, including the colorimetric and electrode methods for nitrogen measurement. The colorimetric method involves chemical reactions that produce a color change proportional to nitrogen concentration, enabling quantification through spectrophotometry. In contrast, the electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to measure nitrogen levels based on electrical potential changes in a solution. Both methods serve critical roles in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and industrial applications, each offering unique advantages in sensitivity and ease of use.</p></p>
