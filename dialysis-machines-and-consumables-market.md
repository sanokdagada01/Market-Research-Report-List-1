<p><strong>The Dialysis Machines and Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis machines and consumables market is primarily segmented into two types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis machines filter blood through an external dialyzer, requiring connection to a patient’s bloodstream, while using consumables like dialysate and catheters. Conversely, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the patient’s abdominal cavity to filter blood, employing solutions like dialysate and catheters tailored for intraperitoneal administration. Both methods address renal failure, influencing treatment choices based on patient needs, lifestyle, and clinical considerations.</p></p>
