<p><strong>The Power Plant Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulverized Fuel</li><li>Circulation Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power plant boilers market primarily includes two types: Pulverized Fuel (PF) and Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) boilers. Pulverized Fuel boilers grind coal into a fine powder to enhance combustion efficiency, allowing for high heat output and reduced emissions. In contrast, Circulating Fluidized Bed boilers use a mixture of sand and fuel particles, enabling efficient combustion at lower temperatures and greater fuel flexibility. Both types cater to various power generation needs, balancing efficiency, emissions control, and operational costs.</p></p>
