<p><strong>The Brass Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open</li><li>Normally Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass solenoid valve market is categorized primarily into two types: normally open (NO) and normally closed (NC). Normally open valves allow fluid flow when de-energized and close when energized, making them suitable for applications requiring constant flow until an action is taken. Conversely, normally closed valves block fluid flow when de-energized and open when energized, ideal for applications needing secure shut-off. These types are crucial in various industries, including irrigation, HVAC, and automation, each serving specific functional needs.</p></p>
