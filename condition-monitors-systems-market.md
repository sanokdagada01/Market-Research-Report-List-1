<p><p>Condition monitoring systems encompass various market types that utilize different technologies to assess equipment health. Vibration analysis detects anomalies in machinery operations. Ultrasound testing evaluates material integrity and thickness. Acoustic emission and airborne ultrasound identify defects by monitoring sound waves. Infrared thermography detects thermal anomalies indicative of potential failures. Motor condition monitoring and Motor Current Signature Analysis (MCSA) evaluate the performance and health of electric motors. Other techniques may include visual inspections and lubrication analysis, contributing to comprehensive monitoring strategies.</p></p>
