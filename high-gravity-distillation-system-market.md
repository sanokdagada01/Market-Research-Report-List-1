<p><p>The High Gravity Distillation System market comprises two main types: Direct Heating Type and Indirect Heating Type. Direct Heating Type utilizes direct heat from flames or hot gases to vaporize the liquid, promoting efficient separation of components. In contrast, the Indirect Heating Type employs heat exchangers to transfer thermal energy, ensuring a more controlled and uniform heating process. Both systems aim to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of distillation processes, catering to various industrial applications and operational requirements.</p></p>
