<p><ul><li>Electronic</li><li>Resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery pack load tester market is divided into two primary types: electronic and resistive testers. Electronic load testers utilize sophisticated circuitry to create a controlled load, allowing for precise measurements of battery performance under various conditions. They are ideal for testing advanced batteries like lithium-ion. In contrast, resistive load testers use resistors to generate a constant load, making them simpler and often more affordable. They are commonly used for basic battery assessment in applications where precision is less critical.</p></p>
