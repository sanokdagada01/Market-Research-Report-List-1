<p><p>The steel seamless pipes market is primarily divided into hot-rolled and cold-rolled categories. Hot-rolled steel seamless pipes are produced at high temperatures, resulting in improved ductility and strength, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, cold-rolled steel seamless pipes are processed at room temperature, offering superior surface finishes and increased dimensional accuracy, ideal for precision applications. Both types serve various industries, including oil and gas, construction, and automotive, meeting diverse structural and fluid transportation needs.</p></p>
