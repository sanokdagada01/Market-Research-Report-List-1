<p><strong>The Natural Oil-Based Polyol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyether Polyols</li><li>Polyester Polyols</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural oil-based polyol market comprises two main categories: polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Polyether polyols are derived from natural oils through processes such as etherification, providing flexibility and water resistance, making them ideal for applications in flexible foams and coatings. Polyester polyols, on the other hand, are obtained by the reaction of natural oils with acids and alcohols, offering enhanced thermal stability and strength, suitable for rigid foams and elastomers. Both types promote sustainability in polymer production.</p></p>
