<p><ul><li>On Casters</li><li>Casterless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The funeral table market can be divided into two main types: caster and casterless tables. Caster tables are equipped with wheels, facilitating easy mobility and repositioning during services and viewings. This feature is particularly advantageous in dynamic funeral settings where flexibility is required. In contrast, casterless tables are stationary, offering stability and a traditional appearance. These are often preferred for more formal ceremonies, where a fixed arrangement is desired. Both types cater to different preferences and ritual requirements in funeral services.</p></p>
