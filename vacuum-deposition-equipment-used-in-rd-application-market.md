<p><p>Vacuum deposition equipment used in R&D applications encompasses several types, including evaporation machines, sputtering machines, and others. Evaporation machines apply thin films by vaporizing material in a vacuum, allowing precise control over layer thickness and composition. Sputtering machines utilize energetic ions to eject atoms from a target material, facilitating the deposition of high-quality films with uniform coverage. Other equipment may include atomic layer deposition and pulsed laser deposition systems, each offering unique methods for material synthesis and characterization in research settings.</p></p>
