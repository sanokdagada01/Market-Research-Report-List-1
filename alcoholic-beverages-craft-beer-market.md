<p><strong>The Alcoholic Beverages Craft Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ales</li><li>Lagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcoholic beverages craft beer market features primarily two types: ales and lagers. Ales are brewed using top-fermenting yeast at warmer temperatures, resulting in a fruity and complex flavor profile. They encompass a variety of styles, including IPAs, stouts, and pale ales. In contrast, lagers utilize bottom-fermenting yeast at cooler temperatures, producing a cleaner, crisper taste. Common lager styles include pilsners and helles. Both categories cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving the growth of the craft beer segment.</p></p>
