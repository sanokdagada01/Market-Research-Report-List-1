<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR)</li><li>Water-Water Energetic Reactor（WWER)</li><li>Pressurized Heavy Water Reactor (PHWR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power market encompasses several generator types, including Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR), Water-Water Energetic Reactors (WWER), and Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR). PWRs use water under pressure as a coolant and neutron moderator, making them the most common type globally. WWERs are similar but primarily used in Eastern Europe and are known for their design simplicity. PHWRs utilize heavy water as a moderator, allowing for natural uranium use, offering operational flexibility and efficiency in various contexts.</p></p>
