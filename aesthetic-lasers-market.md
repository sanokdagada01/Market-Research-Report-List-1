<p><p>The aesthetic lasers market comprises three main types: gas lasers, solid lasers, and semiconductor lasers. Gas lasers, such as CO2 lasers, emit beams through gas mediums, ideal for skin resurfacing and hair removal. Solid lasers use solid-state materials, offering precision and versatility in treatments like tattoo removal and wrinkle reduction. Semiconductor lasers, commonly found in portable devices, are energy-efficient and suitable for various applications, including acne treatment and pigmentation issues. Each type serves distinct aesthetic purposes, catering to diverse cosmetic needs.</p></p>
