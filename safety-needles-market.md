<p><p>The safety needles market encompasses two main types: active safety needles and passive safety needles. Active safety needles require the user to engage a mechanism to shield the needle after use, enhancing safety through deliberate action. Conversely, passive safety needles automatically retract or cover the needle without user intervention, providing immediate safety benefits. Both types aim to reduce needle-stick injuries and improve safety in medical settings, contributing to better healthcare practices and compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
